Family-run sweets shop & dhaba serving authentic Rajasthani sweets, namkeens, and homestyle vegetarian meals. Fresh, hygienic, and crafted the traditional way.

Born from a simple family recipe book, New Bikaner celebrates Rajasthani flavors in every bite. Our sweets — barfis, laddoos, milk cake and more — are slow-cooked and finished with care. The Vaishno dhaba serves hearty vegetarian meals: thalis, paneer specials, tandoori breads and quick snacks. Whether you're ordering a plate or a party pack, we promise true taste and consistent quality.
